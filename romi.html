my favorite food is:

Chocolate souffle

150 grams of bitter dark chocolate
150 grams of butter "Tnuva"
2 eggs
2 egg yolks
Half a cup of sugar
0.25 cup flour
Pack of white chocolate
preparation method:
Preparation:

1. Heat oven to 180 degrees
2. Melt the chocolate and butter in a large bowl.
3.Add the eggs, yolks and sugar to the chocolate and butter mixture and stir well.
4. Add the flour to the mixture and stir until you can not see the flour. The more mixed the less the souffle tastes.
5. Pour the mixture into greased muffins (preferably silicone molds) and place in the oven For only 9 minutes! (This step is very critical too thin and it will just be a normal cake)
6. Remove the souffl? carefully from the baking pan and sprinkle with a little powdered sugar. With appetite!
