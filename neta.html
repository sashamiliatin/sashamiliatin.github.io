my favorite food is:

Crepe

INGREDIENTS:
a cup of flour
Glass of milk / water
Tablespoon oil
2 bags of vanilla sugar
teaspoon of vanilla extract
1 egg
PREPARATION:
Mix everything together until you get a little thick liquid mixture and no lumps.
Fry in a pan with a little oil or margarine,The amount of frying women is according to the size of the frying pan and of course the fry are made to fry both sides of the crepe.
You can serve with jam, maple, ice cream, chocolate or any other idea.
